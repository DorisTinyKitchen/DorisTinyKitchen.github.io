Doris' Tiny Kitchen

Doris' Tiny Kitchen is a branch of Doris' personal website, which aims to share this tiny girl's cooking and baking expreience to others.

Doris was not able to cook before coming to UK, but she had a dramatic improvement in cooking skills after living independently for 6 months. She has learnt many fantanstic and easy receipes from different popular cookers on Youtube, Weibo and mobile applications. To encourage people like her to enjoy cooking, this website is created to provide the most effective cooking information from the internet for new beginners

Currently, the website has three main functions, namely: recipes introduction, calorie calculation and shopping lists production. All the recipes are tested by Doris in person and are guaranteed to be easy to learn and have a great taste.

My dear friend, wish you also fall in love with cooking on day in the no-long distant future.
